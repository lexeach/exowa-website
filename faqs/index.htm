What services EXOWA offer?

Parents can easily create customized tests tailored to your child's unique learning needs. Our platform allows parents to select subjects, difficulty levels, and question types, ensuring a well-rounded assessment experience. Whether you want to reinforce classroom lessons, prepare for exams, or focus on weak areas, our Algorithm-powered system generates question papers that adapt to your child's academic progress. With just a few clicks, you can design tests that challenge and engage, making learning more effective and enjoyable.

Can one subscription will work for diffrent class student?

One subscription will work for one student/class to generate question paper, if there are more than one student then user have to purchase separate subscription for both the students.
