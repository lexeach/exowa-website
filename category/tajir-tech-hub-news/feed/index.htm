What is a Token Burn?

A token burn is the process of permanently removing tokens from circulation by sending them to an inaccessible wallet. This reduces the total supply, creating a deflationary effect, which can potentially increase the value of the remaining tokens. It’s a strategic move that reflects our commitment to strengthening the TJRM token economy and enhancing long-term benefits for our community.

Why We Burned Tokens

From the outset, our vision has been to build a robust and dynamic ecosystem where every stakeholder benefits. By reducing the total supply of TJRM tokens from 1 billion to 900 million, we aim to:

  • Increase Token Scarcity: A reduced supply increases the rarity of TJRM tokens, which can enhance their value.
  • Boost Investor Confidence: Demonstrating our commitment to sustainability and value creation builds trust among investors and users.
  • Strengthen the Ecosystem: Aligning tokenomics with long-term goals ensures a stable and thriving platform for years to come.

The Beginning of a New Era
